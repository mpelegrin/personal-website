Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in Monument, Colorado, after spending my childhood traveling around the world as the daughter of a military officer. My journey into tech has morphed from earning a bachelors degree in psychology and a master degree in social work. As I've worked on increasingly larger scales in social work, I am facinated with how tech can help spread education and resources to more people. I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own sites to reach a larger audience.

That curiosity has opened a door that can never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Entering the world of tech, I am motivated to use my communication and organizational skills from social work to create user-friendly websites and write clean, elegant code.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code
